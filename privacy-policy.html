What We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do NOT use complex analytics tools to monitor your activities
  • We do NOT collect device information, location data, or technical details
  • We do NOT create user profiles or behavioral databases
  • We do NOT share any information with third-party data brokers or analytics companies

You can browse our website, read teachings, use the Sai Chits feature, get daily quotes, and explore worship resources completely anonymously without providing any personal information.
